3x3 Basketball Tournament

The 3x3 Basketball Tournament
is a step forward in promoting
sports and well-being!

Tournament Format

1. Group stage

In the group stage, each team will compete against every other team within their respective groups and the 2 top teams of each group will advance to the knockout stage.

Date: The group stages will be held from the 17th to the 21st of April after school (17:00 - 18:30)

2. Knockout stage

In the knockout stage, the top teams of each group will face other teams in the knockout stage.

Date: The knockout stage will be held in May. More info coming soon.

Please note that the details provided are subject to change based on the number of teams registered or weather conditions, and some specifics may need to be adjusted accordingly.

General Game Rules

We will play with official FIBA 3x3 basketball rules with exceptions.

Exceptions

1. Games are 10 minutes long

2.No shot-clock. Therefore, fouls shall be awarded for time wasting
