Who Are We?

We are the Pupils' Committee of the European School Luxembourg. Promoting the wellbeing of students is a top priority, and we believe that sports plays a crucial role in achieving this goal. Physical activity has diverse benefits, and it also fosters important social and civic values such as team spirit and solidarity. To promote these ideals, we have organized a basketball tournament this year and are considering expanding it in the future.

3x3 Basketball Tournament

The tournament is broken down into two different stages: 1. Group stages and 2. Knockout stage
